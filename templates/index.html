Transform Your Photos

Prityme is your all-in-one smart image editor which helps you to compress, resize and remove background from your photos.

Features

layers_clear

Background Remover

Remove backgrounds automatically using rembg python library. Works great for portraits and product shots while preserving hair and fine edges. No GPU required.

compress

Image Compressor

Choose format, quality and max width to reduce file size by re-encoding at a lower quality — perfect for web or sharing.

photo_size_select_large

Image Resizer

Resize by exact dimensions or scale percentage with aspect-ratio lock. Optionally constrain upscales to preserve quality. Uses Canvas API.
